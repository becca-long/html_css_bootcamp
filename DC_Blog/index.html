Meetup is a social networking and membership site that believes, “Getting together with real people in real life makes powerful things happen. Side hustles become careers, ideas become movements, and chance encounters become lifelong connections. Meetup brings people together to create thriving communities.”

For Houstonians visiting their site, you can find like-minded individuals who are interested in almost any industry, hobby or interest you can think of. There are many tech focused meetups in Houston and it can be a bit overwhelming for newcomers to decide which one is the right one for them.

Certainly, you can choose by interest, but even if you do, which one is right for you. Below are 5 popular tech meetups in Houston, according to our instructors and students anyway. Feel free to check them out, or browse around and find the right one for you!
